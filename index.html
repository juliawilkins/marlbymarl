About

Multi-View Audio Representation Learning is an innovative approach to disentangle and understand complex audio scenes. By leveraging multiple perspectives or "views" of audio data, this research aims to improve the quality of audio-based applications, from source separation to environmental sound analysis.

Research

Explore our ongoing projects and methodologies in audio representation learning.

Project 1: Audio Disentanglement

A study on separating complex soundscapes into distinct components using multi-view learning.

Project 2: Spectrogram Analysis
